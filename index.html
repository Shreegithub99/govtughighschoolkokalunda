About Us

GOVT UG HIGH SCHOOL, KOKALUNDA, GANJAM.

Class - I to X Co- Education

Kokalunda, one remarkable place 8 km distance from Bhanjanagar. Established in the year 1906 and upgraded into secondary school in the year 2011. It is famous for its Goddess Maa Kali and blessed people are enriched in art and traditional culture of dance and songs. About three hundred students are learning in this institution. School is equipped with beautiful library room and science lab. Twelve experienced teachers including Headmaster play vital roles in this noble profession.
